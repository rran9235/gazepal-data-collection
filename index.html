GazePal Data Collection

Hi, Rishi Rangarajan here. For my thesis, I am developing a gaze tracking system to control devices looking at various parts of a computer screen.

I am requesting your help to collect additional data to help my machine learning algorithm generalise better. Crowd-sourcing gaze data will help me create a dataset with a wide variety of eye shapes, colours, head poses, screen sizes, etc.

Kindly request you to spare about 10 minutes and assist me by following the instructions below. The process involves filling in a user data form and recording your eye movements as you follow a marker that moves around the screen.

Instructions:

  1. Enter full-screen on your browser (F11 on Windows).
  2. Fill in the form and download the data as a '.txt' file .
  3. Use the webcam preview to check if your eyes are visible in the video stream.
  4. Click 'Start Recording' once ready to begin data collection.
  5. Remember to grant permission for the program to access your webcam for recording.
  6. Focus your eyes on the red marker and follow it as it moves around the screen.
  7. After the animation is completed, use the 'Download' button to download the recorded video.
  8. Share the downloaded '.mp4' and '.txt' files via email on rran9235@uni.sydney.edu.au
