I require your help to collect additional data to improve my machine learning algorithm. Crowd-sourcing gaze data collection will help collect data for a wide variety of eye shapes, colours, etc. to help my algorithm generalise better. Kindly request you to spare about 10 minutes to assist me by following the instructions below. The application will collect some basic user information from you and record your eye movements as you follow a marker that moves around the screen.

Instructions:

  1. Click 'Next' and fill in the user information form.
  2. Click 'Download' the text file containing form data.
  3. Click 'Next' to be taken to the next page.
  4. Click 'Start Recording' once ready.
  5. Kindly grant permission to access your webcam (in-built or external).
  6. Follow the red marker as it traverses along various screen regions.
  7. Once completed, a 'Download' button will appear. Click on this to download the mp4 video file
  8. Kindly share the video and the text file so I can build my database.
